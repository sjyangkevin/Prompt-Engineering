Find the value of x in the equation 2x / 2 = 10.
Multiply both sides by 2
the equation becomes 2x = 20.
Divide both sides by 2.
x = 10.
The value of x is 10.

Find the value of x in the equation 3x / 3 = 9 + 2x.
Multiply both sides by 3
the equation becomes 3x = 27 + 6x.
Subtract 3x from both sides
the equation becomes 0 = 27 + 3x.
Subtract 27 from both sides
the equation becomes -27 = 3x.
Divide 3 from both sides
the equation becomes -9 = x.
The value of x is -9.
